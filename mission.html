Our Mission
Our mission is to combat economic barriers in the participation of sports by providing resources such as free classes and equipment to children regardless of economic background.
We encourage our community to involve in tennis and empowers kids towards lifetime success through sports. Our desire to give back to the community by reaching disadvantaged, at-risk youth as well as other kids and teens, using tennis and education as a vehicle for success.In addition to youth tennis resources, Time to Tennis provides youth programs such as before-school tennis programs at local schools and enrichment programs, where students can get involved with the tennis community and learn tennis skills.
