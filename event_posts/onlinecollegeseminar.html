Tennis College Recruitment Online Seminar
JULY 15TH
REGISTER CLOSED

We successfully kicked off our week-long summer camp with lots of enjoyment for everyone!
Although we couldn’t host a seminar on tennis recruitment in person, we still enjoyed presenting all the information virtually, at the comfort of everyone’s homes. Our speakers were very knowledge about the specific details they were talking about and we had a wide variety of experiences that the panel talked about. They all had special stories of their own that made their journey so complete and successful. Those speakers include Shreya Mukund, who now attends Case Western Reserve University, Jessica Liang, who is currently at Johns Hopkins University, and Peter Shin from the University of Idaho. All the attendees thoroughly enjoyed the information that they absorbed. The video can be found below!
