School's Out! Beginner Tennis Event
JUNE 17, 2020
REGISTRATION CLOSED

School's Out! Summer is finally here! To kick off the summer, we held an event on June 17th, 2020 for young beginners to start their summer tennis season.
We introduced them to the basics of the game. We ran some fun drills that allowed the children to use their new tennis skills. We also introduced the children to some beginner point play and giving them an introduction to competing in tennis.

The event was a huge success as the kids were given the opportunity of being introduced to all aspects of the game while having a lot of fun! As the summer progresses Time To Tennis will be running more events. Stay tuned!
