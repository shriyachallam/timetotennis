Our Team

THE MAIN INGREDIENT TO SUCCESS IS TEAMWORK!

Sahithi Challam

Co-founder and President

Sahithi is a rising freshman in the gifted International Baccalaureate (IB) diploma program at Interlake High School. She is extermely passionate about tennis and has been working hard to continue to improve her tennis skills. She has been playing competitive tennis for seven years, taking lessons at tennis centers, participating in high-level tournaments, and practicing tennis three times a week. She absolutely loves tennis. She has extensive experience in tennis and teaching tennis applying her skills in Time to Tennis. Sahithi is also an determined dancer who has been learning and performing dance for six years. She enjoys practicing Indian classical dance (Kuchipudi). Additonally, Sahithi loves to do art and help support her community. She was inspired to joing Time to Tennis after noticing the barriers in the participation of sports, especially tennis. She feels extremely privileged to have the opportunities given to her to explore her interests in sports and hopes that Time to Tennis can help others form the same passion that she feels for tennis!

Shriya Challam

Co-founder and Advisor

Shriya Challam is Shriya is a senior in the gifted International Baccalaureate (IB) diploma program at Interlake High School. She is passionate about tennis and loves working hard in her strengths and weakenesses, techniques, and strategies in tennis. Her interest in tennis was first sparked when her dad encouraged her to participate in the sport, tennis, inspiring her to continue playing for ten years, to compete in high-level tournaments, and play for her varsity team in her junior year. She is skilled in both playing tennis and teaching tennis and seeks to tackle challenges in the tennis community. Shriya is also an accomplished dancer. She has been studying and practicing Indian classical dance(Kuchipudi) for nine years. Shriya is determined to overcome economic obstacles and beleives Time to Tennis is the key to help support her community.

Mihir Subramanian

Vice President and Head Coach

Mihir Subramanian is a coach for the Seattle branch who teaches kids the basic fundamentals of tennis, and helps them improve their technique. He loves to work with kids who have the enthusiasm to learn the game, and will encourage them to participate as much as possible.

Matthew Chu

Outreach Manager

Matthew Chu is a sophomore at Bellevue High School in Washington state. He’s played since his parents introduced him to the sport at a young age. He played on his school’s varsity tennis team and is passionate about helping those in his community learn about tennis.

Raj Agarwal

Website Developer

Raj Agarwal is a sophomore in the gifted International Baccalaureate (IB) diploma program at Interlake High School. He is a passionate programmer who enjoys working in all areas of programming. Currently, he is exploring the world of Python to further the interactivity of his website creations. Besides programming, Raj is an avid musician who absolutely loves the Cello. He enjoys finding new music and is currently performing in the Interlake Orchestra. Most of all, Raj loves to help others, so why not help teach the world about the wonders of Tennis!

Jaya Manhas

Social Media Manager and Blog Writer

Jaya Manhas is a student at Mercer Island High School and coaches for the Seattle branch. She started playing tennis at 6 years old and competes regularly in USTA tournaments. She is also on MIHS Girls Varsity tennis team. She plays tennis at least 6 days a week and is thrilled to be a Time To Tennis coach. “Having the opportunity to teach kids the timeless game of tennis is so exciting because tennis is a life long sport. Growing up with tennis has been a great outlet mentally and physically as I have created and met amazing friends. I am excited to reciprocate my knowledge of the game to kids.”
