Thank You!
NOVEMBER 29, 2020

As we are getting ready for Thanksgiving, we would like to give our own thanks to the wonderful donation we recently got. We received 10 kids’ rackets that are greatly going to help so many children be able to play tennis, without needing to purchase any expensive equipment. We are so thankful for all our donors and hope we can connect with even more in the near future!
