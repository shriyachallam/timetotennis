Our First Post!
JANUARY 11, 2020

Hi! Welcome to the TimeToTennis blog! We are a non-profit based in Bellevue, WA dedicated to providing equipment and resources to kids who are interested in learning to play tennis. We have started this blog to share our passion for creating opportunities to learn tennis for young children, regardless of their family's financial circumstances.

Although our team is currently small, we have many ideas in the brewing pot and are ambitiously planning for the future! We hope to raise awareness about community funding for participation in sports. We hope to use donations to provide free equipment and classes for children. Additionally, in the future, we want to partner with other community groups and hold free classes and tournaments to inspire kids to find their passion in tennis! Of course we have many more ideas planned, but we wanted to let you in on our goals. Especially since the members, volunteers, and participants are such a vital role in any nonprofit's success!

We hope this first post has given you an understanding of our mission and purpose. Our blog will have fairly regular postings, and we will constantly be updating our website with information of events and initiatives we are hosting, so please check back soon to get more involved in our projects. We hope to see you soon!
