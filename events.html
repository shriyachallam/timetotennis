    • Future Stars Tennis Camp!
    Bellevue, WA
    July 13th-17th
    REGISTRATION CLOSED

    Time To Tennis held its first week-long tennis camp! The camp was completely free and was held from July 13th-17th from 2-3pm every day at the Interlake High School tennis courts. Each day, the kids were introduced to basic tennis shots and skills to help them create a solid foundation for their game. We also played various games and drills that will help them progress to more advanced tennis skills such as rallying, competition, and point play.
